Gimbap

Description

Gimbap is a Korean dish made from cooked rice and ingredients such as vegetables, and meats that are rolled in gimâ€”dried sheets of seaweed.

Ingredients

  • Dried seaweed sheets
  • Rice
  • Beef skirt steak
  • Carrots
  • Pickled radish
  • Spinach
  • Eggs
  • Garlic
  • Soysauce
  • Brown sugar
  • Salt
  • Sesame oil

Steps

  1. Cook the rice and season with salt and sesame oil and gently mix
  2. Blanch the spinach and season with minced garlic, salt and sesame oil
  3. Saute the thinly sliced carrots and season with salt and sesame oil
  4. Cut the meat into strips and marinate with soysauce, minced garlic, pepper sugar and sesame oil
  5. Cook the meat
  6. Beat the eggs and cook egg strips and season with salt
  7. Cut the Pickled radish into thin slices
  8. Spread the rice evenly on your dried seaweed
  9. Place each ingredient inside and roll up your kimbap
  10. Use some rice as adhesive to close the kimbap
  11. With a plastic glove on, rub some sesame oil on the outer layer and serve cut
