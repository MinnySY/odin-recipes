Ingredients

  • Kimchi
  • Pork belly or any pork
  • sesame oil
  • onions
  • garlic
  • tofu
  • pepper
  • Green Onions
  • Soy Sauce
  • pepper
  • salt

Steps

  1. Cook the pork in a pot and season with pepper, garlic, and salt
  2. Add the kimchi in the pot with the sesame oil and cook at low heat
  3. Add water and some kimchi water and let boil
  4. Add the tofu, onion, green onions and pepper
  5. Add soysauce to taste
