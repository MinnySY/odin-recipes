Tteokbokki

Description

Tteokbokki, or simmered rice cake, is a popular Korean food made from small-sized garae-tteok called tteokmyeon or commonly tteokbokki-tteok. Eomuk, boiled eggs, and scallions are some common ingredients paired with tteokbokki.

Ingredients

    Ingredients

  • Long rice cake
  • Fish cakes
  • Green Onions/Scallions
  • Hard boiled eggs
  • Sesame Seeds

    • Sauce

  • Gochujang
  • Sugar
  • Gochugaru
  • Dried Anchovies
  • Dried kelp
  • Soy Sauce

Steps

  1. Boil dried anchovies and kelp in water for 15 mins in medium high heat without lid
  2. Remove the anchovies and kelp and
  3. Add rice cakes, fish cakes and the sauce ingredients
  4. Stir until the sauce thickens (~10 mins)
  5. Add hard boiled eggs for 2 minutes
  6. Sprinkle Sesame seeds on top
  7. Eat!
